FAQ

What is RiseApp?

RiseApp is a social platform which allows users to attend/create challenges and have a scrape with others via the platform. The users can share and like videos, create new challenges and vote other’s challenges via the platform.

Could you tell more about challenges?

There are 3 challenge types: RiseApp Challenges, Community Challenges (Buckets) and Sponsored Challenges.


RiseApp Challenges

Every day, RiseApp challenges its community with a new topic. It is usually something related to the meaning of the day. (e.g. Surprise Your Mother on mothers day.)


Community Challenges (Bucket)

RiseApp Community can also decide what the next day's challenge is going to be. Everyone can vote for the ideas in the “Bucket” but only the users with enough badges can submit their ideas for voting.


Sponsored Challenges

One of three daily challenges is booked for Sponsored Partners. Partners can launch their ad campaigns as a challenge and encourage people with giveaways to make a video to spread their ad campaigns.


Each and every challenge is active for 24 hours for submitting, 48 hours for voting.

Users can:
- Submit for today's challenge
- Vote for today's challenge
- Vote for yesterdays challenge

Does it cost anything to become a member?

No. You can download the app, sign up and start sharing challenges right away!
